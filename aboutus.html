Mission & Vision

Wooden Bakery's mission is to ascend as the top bakery choice in the Middle East and attain a prominent global position. We strive to establish ourselves as the preferred bakery destination while reinforcing our brand in the industry.

This is accomplished by consistently surpassing customer expectations, delivering superior products and services, and exceeding customer satisfaction at every interaction.

Our Long Term Vision & Goals

Our aim is to build a revered national and international franchise network that exceeds the value of its individual components. Through franchising, we leverage our partners' strengths to achieve our shared objectives. Franchise owners benefit from comprehensive training, a tested business model, tailored support, a robust brand identity, and unparalleled prospects for success.

We are dedicated to sharing the inherent rewards of our business.

Frequently Asked Questions:

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes, we offer a variety of gluten-free products. Please inquire with our staff for more details.

Can I place a custom cake order?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom cakes for all occasions. Contact us to discuss your design and flavor preferences.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer delivery services within our local area. Additional fees may apply.

What are your opening hours?

Our bakery is open from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Saturday.

Are your ingredients locally sourced?

Yes, we prioritize using locally sourced ingredients whenever possible to ensure freshness and support local farmers.

Do you have vegan options available?

Yes, we offer a selection of vegan baked goods. Check our menu for options.

Can I order in bulk for events?

Certainly! We accept bulk orders for events such as weddings, corporate gatherings, and parties. Contact us in advance to discuss your requirements.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available for orders canceled within 2 days of the scheduled pickup or delivery date. Please contact us for assistance with refunds.
